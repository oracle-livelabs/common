## AI-Generated Content Guide

### 1. Introduction
AI-generated content has the potential to increase efficiency, but it often needs refinement to meet high-quality standards. This document provides guidelines to ensure AI-generated technical marketing content is **concise, engaging, and accurate**, avoiding fluff, weak phrasing, and unprovable claims.

### 2. Key Goals for AI-Generated Content
✅ **Concise** – No filler, just strong, direct language.  
✅ **Active** – Uses strong verbs, avoids fluff.  
✅ **Precise** – No vague claims or empty adjectives.  
✅ **Engaging** – If possible, story-driven.  

### 3. AI Prompting Guidelines

#### How to Write Effective Prompts
To generate high-quality content, use prompts that enforce clarity and precision. Examples:
- **Force Active Voice & Strong Verbs:**  
  - *"Write a one-paragraph summary of [feature]. Use short, direct sentences. Avoid adverbs and weak verbs like 'providing' or 'leveraging.' Make every word count."*
- **Prevent Wordiness:**  
  - *"Describe [feature] in under 30 words. Do not use adjectives like ‘cutting-edge’ or ‘innovative.’ Every word must add meaning."*
- **Remove Prepositional Phrase Overload:**  
  - *"Rewrite the following passage in 20% fewer words. Remove unnecessary prepositional phrases and replace weak verbs with stronger ones."*
- **Encourage Storytelling:**  
  - *"Explain how [feature] helped a business solve a real problem. Use a simple narrative format: (1) The problem, (2) The solution, (3) The outcome. Keep it concise."*

#### Common Pitfalls & How to Avoid Them
- **Wordiness** → Trim unnecessary words.
- **Weak or vague verbs** → Use strong action verbs.
- **Overuse of prepositional phrases** → Simplify sentence structure.
- **Generic or uninspired tone** → Add context or a light narrative.
- **Inaccuracies or unprovable claims** → Ensure every claim is fact-based.

### 4. Review Checklist for AI-Generated Content
Before finalizing AI-generated content, ensure it meets these criteria:

#### Clarity & Conciseness
✅ Is every sentence necessary? Eliminate fluff.  
✅ Are unnecessary prepositional phrases removed?  
✅ Can any sentence be shortened without losing meaning?  

#### Strong, Active Verbs
✅ Are weak verbs like *providing, leveraging, enabling* replaced with stronger ones?  
✅ Does every verb drive action?  

#### Logical Flow & Engagement
✅ Does the opening sentence hook the reader with a real-world problem or context?  
✅ Is the structure logical? (Problem → Solution → Outcome)  

#### No Marketing Fluff
✅ Are vague words like *cutting-edge, innovative, seamlessly* present? Remove them.  
✅ Does every claim have a factual basis? No exaggerations.  

#### Accuracy & Technical Rigor
✅ Are all technical features correctly described? Cross-check.  
✅ Are there any unprovable claims? If so, rework or remove them.  

#### External Constraints
✅ Do not use any of the following words or their roots or synonyms: *guarantee, insure, ensure, assure*.
✅ Avoid the sentence pattern *<adverb> <weak verb> <noun clause> <gerund> <noun clause>*.

### 5. Implementation Plan
To ensure consistency, teams should follow these steps:
1. **Apply AI Prompts** – Use structured prompts when generating content.
2. **Review Output Using the Checklist** – Eliminate fluff, verify facts, and improve clarity.
3. **Refine & Iterate** – Adjust based on feedback and performance.
4. **Assign Responsibilities** – Ensure key team members are responsible for reviewing and improving AI-generated content.

### 6. Continuous Improvement
- **Measure Success** – Define metrics to evaluate content quality and engagement.
- **Feedback Loops** – Regularly collect input from team members.
- **Iterate** – Continuously refine prompts and review strategies based on what works best.

This document provides a structured approach to ensuring AI-generated technical marketing content is clear, engaging, and precise. By following these guidelines, teams can create compelling content that aligns with business objectives while maintaining high editorial standards.
