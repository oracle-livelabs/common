# Prose Gate

Apply this gate after structural fixes and before final delivery.

## Required Style
- Use direct, active voice.
- Prefer short sentences. Aim for under 20 words when practical.
- Use strong verbs.
- Keep claims specific and defensible.
- Make instructions concrete and easy to execute.

## Disallowed Or Discouraged Patterns
- contractions in final scored prose,
- em dashes,
- filler adjectives,
- generic AI phrasing,
- vague praise,
- unsupported superlatives,
- weak verbs when stronger verbs exist.

Avoid words with roots or close synonyms of:
- `guarantee`
- `insure`
- `ensure`
- `assure`

Rewrite the sentence instead of leaning on these verbs.

## Sentence-Level Review

For each file, look for:
- long setup before the main verb,
- stacked nouns,
- repetitive sentence openings,
- hedging that weakens instructions,
- abstract claims without evidence,
- bloated transitions,
- generic outcome language.

## Workshop Voice

- Sound like a precise instructor, not a marketer.
- Favor concrete outcomes over hype.
- Keep tone calm, authoritative, and specific.
- Match the source workshop's level of technical depth.
