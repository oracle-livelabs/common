# Need Help?

## Introduction

Learn how to get help when developing your workshops. We provide several resources to ensure you receive the assistance you need.

Estimated Time: x

### Objectives
- Learn about LiveLabs helpful resources

## Option 1: Use Self-Service Learning Resources

1. Read this LiveLabs Step by Step guide for complete and updated information about LiveLabs workshops.

2. Ensure your workshop follows all LiveLabs standards.

## Option 2: Send an Email

1. Send an email to your stakeholders or council group for workshop advice.

2. Email your questions to our shared emailbox: *livelabs-help-db_us@oracle.com*. We respond within 1-2 business days.

## Option 3: Attend Office Hours

1. Attend office hours for one-on-one help: [American time zone every Tuesday 8 AM (PST)](https://bit.ly/LivelabsOfficeHrs) or [EMEA time zone every Wednesday 9 AM (GMT)](https://bit.ly/emealivelabsofficehrs).

## Option 4: Use Slack Channel

1. Join our Slack channel #workshop-authors-help for immediate help.
