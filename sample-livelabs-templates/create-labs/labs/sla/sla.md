# LiveLabs SLAs

| Process | SLA | Notes |
|---------|-----|-------|
| GitHub PR Review | 1 business day | LiveLabs admin team reviews PRs |
| Workshop Submission Review | 2-3 business days | Council reviews and approves or requests more information |
| Workshop Approval (Council) | 3 business days | Follow up if no response after 3 business days |
| Workshop Approval (No Council Response) | 5 business days | Escalation if council does not react |
| Stakeholder QA Verification | 2 business days | Stakeholders verify Self QA submission |
| Move to Completed Status | 1 business day | After stakeholder QA verification |
| Workshop Publishing | 1 business day | Once workshop team requested publishing request in Publishing Tab in WMS |
| Green Button Lite Creation | 1 business day | Automatic creation after approval |
| Custom Green Button to Production | Depends on requirements | After confirmation email received |
| Event Code Request Review | 1 business day | LiveLabs team reviews request |
| WMS to Production Sync | Within 1 day | Changes reflected in production |
| Email Support Response | 1-2 business days | Via livelabs-help-db_us@oracle.com |
| Slack Support Response | Same day | Via #livelabs-authors-help channel |
{: title="LiveLabs SLAs"}

> **Note:** Do not submit requests for pull request merges on our Slack channel. Do not send direct messages to team members regarding these requests. If your request remains incomplete after the service level agreement period ends, post your inquiry in the #livelabs-authors-help channel.
